# Simulacra

## North Star

The engine is the product. The gameplay is just the excuse to interact with it. If the simulation feels fake or shallow, no amount of game design saves it. If the simulation feels real, players will forgive almost anything else.

## Project Overview

Simulacra is a 3D physics-based puzzle/engineering game with a purpose-built engine. Three simulation domains—fluid, electromagnetic, and rigidbody—are deeply coupled so that energy and forces flow freely between them. The cross-domain interactions (conductive fluids completing circuits, eddy current braking, MHD pumping, induction, resistance heating driving convection) ARE the game mechanics.

## Gameplay

Combination of "always live" engineering (no build/test separation—the world is always running) and curated Zachtronics-style puzzles that teach players to discover and exploit cross-domain physics interactions.
